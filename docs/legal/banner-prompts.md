## Shared Visual Direction

- Brand mood: premium, disciplined, modern and confident.
- Palette: matte black, charcoal, graphite and controlled burnt-orange accents.
- Photography: realistic DSLR / cinematic editorial quality.
- Lighting: soft directional light with deep but readable shadows.
- Layout: right-weighted subject and left-side negative space.
- Avoid: visible text, logos, watermarks, stock-photo smiles, bright gym colours, cyberpunk effects, excessive smoke, distorted hands and obvious AI artefacts.

## Optional Mobile Crop Direction

For mobile versions, regenerate at **1080 × 1350 px** with the subject centred in the lower half and clean negative space in the upper third for the page heading. Keep the same materials, lighting and colour direction.
